Formules de base

  • \( (u+v)' = u'+v' \)
  • \( (uv)' = u'v + uv' \)
  • \( (u/v)' = (u'v - uv') / v^2 \)
  • \( (u^n)' = nu^{n-1}u' \)
  • \( (\ln u)' = u'/u \)
  • \( (e^u)' = u'e^u \)
  • \( (\sin u)' = u'\cos u \)
  • \( (\cos u)' = -u'\sin u \)
